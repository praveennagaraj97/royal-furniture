Warranty

Investing in Royal Manor is an investment in generations of craftsmanship. Our warranty serves as a testament to the enduring quality of our curated pieces.

Frequently Asked Questions

All signature collections from Royal Manor are covered by a 10-year structural warranty and a 2-year upholstery warranty. Heritage pieces may carry extended lifetime protection on specific joinery.
Our warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship, including frame structural integrity, spring failure, and significant joint separation under normal residential use.
Contact your dedicated Curator or our Boutique via the registry. You will need your original order number and a brief description of the issue accompanied by high-resolution photographs.
The warranty does not cover natural variations in wood grain or leather scarring, damage from improper cleaning, environmental exposure (excessive humidity/sunlight), or modifications performed by third parties.
Our warranty is tied to the original purchaser and the delivery address. However, Heritage Registry members may transfer their warranty during a certified antique resale through our boutique.
