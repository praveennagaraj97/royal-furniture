Returns Policy

A Commitment to Excellence.

We understand that curating your perfect home is a journey. Our returns process is designed to be as seamless and refined as our furniture pieces.

Concierge Assistance

Need help with a return? Our specialists are available 24/7 for white-glove support.

All standard collection pieces are eligible for return within 30 calendar days of delivery. This allows you to experience the piece in your space and ensure it meets our heritage standards of comfort and aesthetic.
Items must be returned in their original, pristine condition. All bespoke protective packaging, authentication certificates, and heritage tags must be intact. Pieces showing signs of use, alterations, or damage will not be eligible for a full refund.
Due to the complex logistics of high-end furniture transport, a 15% restocking fee applies to all returns. Original delivery fees are non-refundable. For White Glove pickup services, additional logistics fees may be deducted from the final credit.
To begin, log into your Royal Manor account and navigate to 'Order Heritage'. Select the item and click 'Request Boutique Return'. A concierge representative will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a specialized inspection and pickup.
Please note that 'Bespoke' and 'Commissioned' pieces—crafted specifically to your fabric or timber specifications—are final sale and cannot be returned once production has commenced.
